### Required when:
- The dataset supports a publication  
- Data were produced using public funding  
- Data contain no sensitive personal information  
- Anonymisation is possible without harming integrity  

### Not required when:
- Sensitive data cannot be anonymised  
- GDPR or privacy would be violated  
- Trade secrets or commercial info are involved  
- Release would compromise scientific strategy  

### Why Zenodo?
- FAIR compliant  
- DOI assignment  
- Long-term preservation  
- DigiWELL official Zenodo community  
- Recognised by journals and Open Access policies
