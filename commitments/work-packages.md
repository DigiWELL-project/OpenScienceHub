---
title: "Open Science Commitments by Work Package"
---

## WP1 – Wellbeing & Digital Technologies
- Preregistration of longitudinal and EMA studies  
- Preprints where allowed  
- Open Peer Review  
- Citizen Science workshops  

## WP2 – Experimental Studies
- Full preregistration for each experiment  
- Upload anonymised datasets to OSF with DOI  
- Upload README + workflow files  

## WP3 – EMA & Interventions
- Preregistration of 1 EMA + 2 RCT studies  
- Multiple Citizen Science activities  
- SOPs for EMA and intervention studies  

## WP4 – eMental Health Interventions
- OSF / ClinicalTrials.gov preregistration  
- Open Peer Review where available  

## WP5 – Applied Outputs & Policy Impact
- Open Peer Review  
- Citizen Science for questionnaire development and apps  
- SOPs and detailed documentation  

## WP6 – Complex Data & Statistical Methods
- Preregistration of methodological papers  
- Open Peer Review  
- Release of scripts for reproducibility
