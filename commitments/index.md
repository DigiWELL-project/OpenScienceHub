---
title: "Open Science Commitments"
---

The DigiWELL project fully implements Open Science principles:

- Mandatory Open Access (91% with APC funding; others under Read & Publish)
- Optional practices: preregistration, preprints, open peer review, Citizen Science
- Sharing methodologies, algorithms, software, SOPs
- Reproducibility: archiving protocols and analysis scripts
- Public involvement: participatory tool development
