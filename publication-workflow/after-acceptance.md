title: "After Your Article is Accepted"
---

## Upload Accepted Manuscript
Upload the accepted manuscript or final PDF to **Zenodo** and select the **DigiWELL Community**.

## Set the Correct Licence
Recommended licence: **CC BY 4.0**

## Upload Supporting Datasets
Upload dataset(s) to Zenodo if they support the publication and can be shared.

## Inform the Administrative Team
They will add the article to the OP JAK indicator lists ("soupisky").
