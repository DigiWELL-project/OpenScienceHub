## Authorship
At least one author must be a member of the DigiWELL research team.

## Mandatory Acknowledgement
Add the following acknowledgement to your manuscript:

> “Research of Excellence on Digital Technologies and Wellbeing CZ.02.01.01/00/22_008/0004583,” which is co-financed by the European Union.

## Open Access Compliance
Ensure the journal permits:
- Open Access  
- Archiving the accepted manuscript (ideally CC BY 4.0)

## Check Your OP JAK Indicator
Confirm whether the output fits one of the indicators:
- **210 181 — Conference contributions**  
- **214 021 — Publications**  
- **214 022 — Specific types of publications**  
- **214 026 — Q1 publications**  
- **214 023 — Publications with international co-authorship**  
- **214 024 — Publications with business co-authorship**

## Data Preparation
Identify datasets and ensure anonymisation and documentation.
