# Publication Workflow

This page summarises the required steps **before submitting a manuscript** and **after the publication is accepted**. The workflow follows **OP JAK requirements** and **DigiWELL Open Science policies**.

---

## Before Submitting Your Manuscript – Checklist

### 1. Authorship
- At least one author must be a member of the **DigiWELL research team**.

### 2. Mandatory Acknowledgement
Include the following acknowledgement in your manuscript:

> “Research of Excellence on Digital Technologies and Wellbeing CZ.02.01.01/00/22_008/0004583,”
> which is co-financed by the European Union.

### 3. Open Access Compliance
Ensure that the selected journal allows:
- Open Access publishing, or
- Archiving of the accepted manuscript (preferably under **CC BY 4.0**).

### 4. OP JAK Indicator Check
Confirm whether the publication fits one of the OP JAK indicators:
- **210 181** — Conference contributions
- **214 021** — Publications
- **214 022** — Specific types of publications
- **214 026** — Q1 publications
- **214 023** — Publications with international co-authorship
- **214 024** — Publications with business co-authorship

### 5. Data Preparation
- Identify all datasets related to the publication
- Ensure proper **anonymisation** (if applicable)
- Prepare basic documentation (README, metadata)

---

## After Your Article Is Accepted

### 6. Upload Accepted Manuscript
- Upload the accepted manuscript or final PDF to **Zenodo**
- Select the **DigiWELL Community**

### 7. Set the Correct Licence
- Recommended licence: **CC BY 4.0**

### 8. Upload Supporting Datasets
- Upload supporting dataset(s) to Zenodo if:
  - they are relevant to the publication, and
  - they can be legally and ethically shared

### 9. Inform the Administrative Team
- Notify the administrative team
- They will register the output in OP JAK indicator lists ("soupisky")

---

## Summary
This checklist is intended to minimise administrative issues, ensure OP JAK compliance, and support DigiWELL’s Open Science commitments. When in doubt, consult the Open Science team **before submission**.
